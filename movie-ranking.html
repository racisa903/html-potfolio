Best Must Watch Movies EVER!!!

By Rachel Miriam Isaac


#1 Mitchells vs Machines

A funny movie about how a family of four tries to manage when robots take over.

#2 Home

The story of a left out alien, whose pals are trying to take over the world. He befreinds a human girl, who helps him stop his pals from taking over the world.

Nicky, Ricky, Dicky, and Dawn

This is a series, but I hope it still counts. A family of six encounters many different stages in life, but the way the deal with them is true comedy.
